HeadShot

Your digital presence is an important aspect in today's world. A great photo on social media platforms can open doors by making you stand out from the crowd. Here at Headshot, I specialise in capturing you in your best light to make your social media profile photo shine.

About Me

My name's Alan and I'm a photogtapher and photo retoucher based in Kerry, Ireland. Headshot photography is my passion and I love having the opportunity to capture a moment when someone is being their true self.


The key to getting a good headshot photo, is being able to make your subject feel relaxed and at ease. After years of practice, I have developed a style that allows people to be themselves when in front of my camera.


I also love to edit photos. I do this using photoshop to retouch photos which is similar to dodging and burning that used to be done for many years in dark rooms of photography studios. With photoshop, I have a lot more tools to help me bring the best out of photographs.

Retouching

I also offer a photo retouching service.


Using photoshop, I can fix up any skin blemishes or imperfections such as red marks or acne. I also use dodging and burning to shape the light on the subject, in order to highlight their features.


Have a look at the image to see what kind of impact retouching can have in a before and after showing what photo retouching can achieve. It helps to make the subject shine and stand out in the image.
